Our Company started back in 2010, We started in Kuwait and We became the grandfather of delevery companies in the small country. In 2015 Microsoft offered 1.7 million Dollars for this company, The owners declined. In one month we expanded to Iraq and Saudi Arabia, then to Bahrain and Qatar. in 2019 we were in over 25 different countries! Now we are world wide and we have over one Million employees! There are other companies that do this in other countries but we are the Only ones in the middle east!
